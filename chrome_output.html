    Beschrijving

    Abraham Benjamin de Villiers is a South African former international cricketer. He is regarded as one of the greatest batters of his generation. de Villiers was named as the ODI Player of the Year three times during his 15-year international career.
    Geboren: 17 februari 1984 (41 jaar), Pretoria, Zuid-Afrika
    Huwelijkspartner: Danielle de Villiers (geh. 2013)
    Rugnummer: 17 (Royal Challengers Bangalore / Batsman, Wicketkeeper) Meer tonen
    Einde carrire: 2021
    Ouders: Millie De Villiers, Abraham B de Villiers
